Home / Croffle

Ingredients

  • 1 package of pre-made croissant dough or puff pastry
  • Butter (for greasing the waffle iron)
  • Optional toppings: powdered sugar, maple syrup, fresh berries, whipped cream, or your favorite spreads like Nutella or jam

Preparation

  1. 1. Prepare the Waffle Iron:
    • Preheat your waffle iron to a medium setting.
    • Lightly grease the waffle iron with butter to prevent sticking.
  2. 2. Prepare the Croissant Dough:
    • If using pre-made croissant dough, unroll the dough and separate it into the pre-cut triangles. If using puff pastry, cut it into squares that will fit your waffle iron.
    • If you want smaller croffles, cut the triangles or squares into smaller pieces.

Instruction

  1. 1. Cook the Croffles:
    • Place a piece of dough onto the heated waffle iron. Close the lid and press down slightly to ensure the dough cooks evenly.
    • Cook for about 3 to 5 minutes or until the croffle is golden brown and crispy.

Finishing

    Carefully remove the croffle from the waffle iron and place it on a plate.
    Serve hot with your choice of toppings like powdered sugar, syrup, berries, whipped cream, or a spread of your choice.
