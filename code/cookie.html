Jammy Cookie

𐙚

Home / Jammy Cookie

Ingredients

  • 225 grams unsalted butter, softened
  • 150 grams granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 280 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Jam of your choice

Preparation

  1. 1. Preheat Oven and Prepare Pans:
    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  2. 2. Make the Cookie Dough:
    • In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar together with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
    • Mix in the vanilla extract.
    • Gradually blend in the flour and salt until the mixture comes together to form a dough.

Instruction

  1. 1. Shape and Fill the Cookies:
    • Roll the dough into 1-inch (2.5 cm) balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet, spaced about 2 inches apart.
    • Use your thumb or the back of a small spoon to make an indentation in the center of each cookie.
    • Fill each indentation with about 1/2 teaspoon of your chosen jam.
  2. 2. Bake:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
    • Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Finishing

    Serve the cookies once cooled, or store them in an airtight container.
    These cookies are perfect for holidays, special occasions, or just a delightful treat with tea or coffee.
