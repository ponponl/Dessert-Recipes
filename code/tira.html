Tiramisu

Home / Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups strong espresso
  • 2 tablespoons rum or coffee-flavored liqueur
  • 7 ounces ladyfingers
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Dark chocolate shavings or cocoa powder

Preparation

  1. 1. Prepare the Coffee Mixture:
    • Mix the cooled espresso with rum or coffee liqueur if using.
  2. 2. Make the Mascarpone Filling:
    • In a large bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until well blended and creamy.
    • Add mascarpone cheese to the yolk mixture and beat until smooth.
  3. 3. Whip the Cream:
    • In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks.
    • Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until combined. Be careful not to deflate the cream.

Instruction

  1. 1. Assemble the Tiramisu:
    • Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture, making sure not to soak them too long.
    • Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers at the bottom of a serving dish.
    • Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
    • Repeat with another layer of soaked ladyfingers and top with the remaining mascarpone mixture.
    • Smooth the top and dust generously with cocoa powder.
  2. 2. Chill:
    • Cover and refrigerate the tiramisu for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld.

Finishing

    Before serving, sprinkle with dark chocolate shavings or additional cocoa powder.
